About Me

I am a passionate artist dedicated to creating realistic portrait drawings using pencil and charcoal. With years of experience in capturing facial expressions, light, and shadow, I bring each portrait to life with technical precision and artistic sensitivity.

My work focuses on the intricate details that make each face unique—the play of light across the skin, the depth in the eyes, and the subtle expressions that convey emotion. Whether you're looking for a personal portrait or a commission, I'm here to create something truly special.
